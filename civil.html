Civil Engineering

Approved Research Center for Ph.D. programme recognized by the University of Mumbai. Offers wide range of electives at UG level which include: Environmental Engineering, Geotechnical Engineering, Structural Engineering and Transportation Engineering. Faculty members represent the university on various committees, such as Board of Studies, Result Moderation Committee, Syllabus Committee. A faculty member represents the Board of Editorial and Reviewers for various reputed international journals. All the 12 laboratories are standardized as per the norms specified by bureau of Indian standards and are well equipped with the state-of–art equipments/instruments/ machineries. Actively runs Testing and Consultancy Cell CEDTCC and is associated with several projects of national importance. Maintains a separate departmental library for ready reference to the students. The final year B.E result is 88.15 % with 78 securing distinction in the merit list in the academic year 2015-16. In the same year two research scholars of DMCE Dr.Smita Patil and Dr.V.B.Ashtankar are awarded Ph.D degree in Civil Engineering under the guidance of Dr.H.S Chore. In the academic year 2016-17 two more research scholars of DMCE Dr. S.K.Mukherjee and Dr.Vidya Patil are awarded Ph.D degree under the guidance of Dr.A.R.Katti and Dr.H.S.Chore respectively.

Welcome to the Department of Civil Engineering, which has been doing the pioneering works in academics from 1988, aligning with the demanding standards of Mumbai University and AICTE. I trust that no matter, the ever demanding needs of the industry and research, we prepare the students for all challenges. The thrust of our department is to advance civil engineering learning, innovation, and absolute commitment besides the evolving responsibility of a global organization.

  • Learning:

    We offer several elective subjects of student’s choice to deliver knowledge and expertise across a wide spectrum. Academic activities are enriched by frequent field visits, projects, seminars and workshops.

  • Innovation:

    Faculty and student research reflects our strong on-going commitment to create more innovative, resilient, sustainable, and holistic solutions for an ever-changing world.

  • Commitment:

    Civil engineering touches every aspect of the society and we have an absolute obligation to ensure our teaching remain responsive to societal needs. We work closely with, and involve, our local, state and national constituents, the professional community, alumni and friends.

  • Vision:

    To achieve excellent standards of quality education by keeping pace with the rapidly changing technologies all over the world with help of technical manpower of civil engineers of this department, who will be having capabilities of accepting new challenges in meeting global standards.

  • Mission:

    M1:To impart quality education in civil engineering to raise satisfaction level of all stake holders.
    M2: To provide state of the art resources that contributes to congenial learning environment.
    M3: To have regular interaction with industry and offer solutions to their problem.
    M4: To establish centres of excellence in emerging areas of research, development and consultancy for industrial and societal needs of the nation.
    M5: To create competent professionals who are trained in design and development of civil engineering systems and finding a solution to community, regional and global problems and accept new challenges in rapidly changing technology.
