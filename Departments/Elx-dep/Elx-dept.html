Electronics Engineering

Electronics department is one of the primordial department of this institute. It was established in the year 1988. Our department has a fine blend of a team of qualified and experienced faculty and staff members and we are striving hard continuously to improve upon the quality of education and to maintain its position of leadership in Engineering and Technology. The Department also strives to offer its students excellent instructional and educational opportunities in Electronics Engineering, thus providing a durable technical foundation in an environment of rapid technical change. It has IETE student chapter and EESA to nurture and culture the talent and skills of the students. In a nutshell, the department is well nurtured to cater the needs of education for the career enhancement of students from both technical as well as social aspects. Wish you all a happy learning here.
