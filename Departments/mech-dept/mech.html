Mechanical Engineering

• Offers post graduate programme with specialization in CAD/CAM and Robotics. • Approved Research Center for Ph.D. programme recognized by the University of Mumbai. • 12 fully equipped laboratories and in-house computer center with 3D modeling and computational software such as AUTOCAD, IDEA, ANSYS, CREO, Lab View, and Visual Basic. • Well equipped workshop with advanced machines like CNC Turning and Milling centers. Various sponsored projects from professional institutions like ISHRAE are undertaken for indigenous development of the test rigs. • Departmental library with more than 500 text and reference books. Bagged 2nd and 5th rank in the university final year merit list for the academic year 2013-14 and 2014-15 respectively. • Final year results of the department are consistently above 85%. • Departmental student chapters include ISHRAE (Indian society of Heating, refrigerating and air conditioning Engineers), ASHRAE (American society of Heating, refrigerating and air conditioning Engineers) and SAE (Society of Automotive Engineers). • Students organization the Forum of Mechanical Engineering Students (FOMES) regularly conducts various technical events.

Datta Meghe College of Engineering

Airoli, Navi Mumbai

Welcome to the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Datta Meghe College of Engineering, Airoli. The Department of Mechanical engineering is established in1993. Department offers B.E. course in Mechanical Engineering. Department started M.E. Program in CAD/CAM and Robotics with the intake of 18 students in 2011. Again in subsequent year Department has started Ph.D. Programme with the intake of 10.


Mechanical engineering is a discipline of engineering that applies the principles of engineering, for design, analysis, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems. A team of highly qualified and experienced staff is the major strength of the department.


Department aims at preparing students with a sound engineering knowledge and a multi-disciplinary approach, imparting high quality and industry oriented curriculum. To accomplish this, Department organizes large number of co-curricular and extracurricular activities such as industrial visits, design & poster competitions, technical paper presentations, guest lectures on recent trends in mechanical engineering, soft skills etc. The Department is associated with 3 major professional student chapters viz. SAE (Society of Automotive Engineers) , ISHRAE(Indian society of Heating, refrigerating and air conditioning Engineers) and ASHRAE (American society of Heating, refrigerating and air conditioning Engineers)which help students to build their technical as well as professional skills and add value in the process of acquiring knowledge. Student’s organization the Forum of Mechanical Engineering Students (FOMES) regularly conducts various technical events. Department promotes various research and development activities initiated by its staff and the students.

  • Vision:

    To achieve academic excellence in Mechanical Engineering by developing complete personality of the student and instilling high level of discipline to make them globally recognized technocrats

  • Mission:

    To impart quality education emphasizing industry oriented curriculum to prepare students with a sound engineering knowledge and a multi-disciplinary approach Fostering effective teaching/learning environment to prepare the students in the practice of discipline Encouraging the spirit of entrepreneurship and networking with industries for technology transfers and commercialization Engaging faculties to exemplify excellence in the pursuit and delivery of knowledge.
