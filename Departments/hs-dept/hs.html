Dr. Henry Aruna

HOD of Humanities and Sciences Department

Datta Meghe College of Engineering

Airoli, Navi Mumbai

A Warm welcome to the Department of Humanities & Sciences at Datta Meghe College of Engineering, Airoli.

“No great building can be made on a weak foundation.A solid foundation alone can build a superstructure.” The Department of Humanities and Sciences with its experienced and well qualified staff,laboratories and departmental library strive to create a strong foundation for the young aspiring technocrats to contribute for the sustainable development of the country. I wish all my students to grow ethically correct and have joyful journey to acquire value based education.

  • Vision:

    To provide strong foundation by imparting knowledge and social sensibility for overall growth and development of students.

  • Mission:

    Laying a strong foundation by introducing subjects relating to Basic Sciences and Humanities and providing opportunities to practice,perform and experiments in basic engineering subjects. Encouraging them to achieve goals by sharpening their skills and aiding to improve their personality to overcome weaknesses. Provide an amicable environment to develop students’ ability to converse rationally, analytically and innovatively in ways that are appropriate across the disciplines.
